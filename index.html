    01

    Lee Kjong

    Dream Sounds

    Award-winning Music Producer

    Lee Kjong is an award-winning music producer based in Los Angeles, California. He has become widely recognized for crafting music that captures the soul of its listeners with a unique combination of classic and modern music. With his highly trained ear and technical knowledge, he has produced music for a number of top musical acts and short films. His dedication to creating music that appeals to all audiences made Lee one of the most sought-after music producers in the industry. From adding depth and texture to each song he produces to experimenting with new sounds, Kjong's music continues to captivate music lovers around the world.

    02

    Sam Rasco

    WonderSong

    Multi-Instrumentalist & Music Producer

    Sam Rasco is a music producer and multi-instrumentalist from New York, New York. After studying music composition at the Manhattan School of Music, he moved to Los Angeles where his music career took off. He has experience with piano, guitar, bass, drums, and vocal coaching which he gained working for various music companies in LA. In addition to teaching music lessons online and working as an independent music producer, Sam creates music for television shows, commercials, and video games. His musical production style blends jazz-influenced rhythms with modern Hip Hop beats. With his creative soundscapes and innovative approach to music production, Sam brings an unrivaled energy to any project—and audience—he works with.

    03

    Sara Hodges

    Palm Tree Studios

    Co-founder & Songwriter

    Sara Hodges, songwriter from Miami, Florida, is an up-and-coming artist to watch. She is a multi-genre songwriter and has written for multiple bands and solo artists. Her songwriting portfolio includes original songs that have been featured on music streaming platforms worldwide. She has been invited to give a presentation about songwriting at this year's music conference and is an exciting voice in the modern music scene. There's no doubt that she will have some inspiring words of wisdom for those in attendance.

    04

    Joe Miller

    Soulfull Music

    Audio Engineer & Music Producer

    Joe Miller is an incredibly talented music producer and audio engineer from Charlotte, North Carolina. Joe has been producing music for over 10 years and his music production style is unique and often recognizable. He has worked with some of the biggest music artists in North America and he always remains composed yet creative when working to bring out the best of music compositions. In 2016, Joe won a Grammy award for his work on the album "The Perfect Storm" by famed artist Wayne Marlowe. As if that was not enough, he also earned a platinum-selling single with his released track “Frequencies”. His hard work and dedication make Joe one the top music producers in Charlotte, not just in music composition but also in sound engineering.
